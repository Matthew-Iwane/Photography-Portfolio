Nikon D810

Multi-CAM 3500FX AF sensor that uses 51 focus points, including 15 cross-type sensors, 11 of which work all the way to f/8, plus a new Group Area AF. Internal vibration has been nearly eliminated with a newly designed sequencer mechanism and Electronic Front Curtain Shutter system.

  • Optical Zoom: 46x
  • Minimum Aperture: 22
  • 5.75 x 3.23 x 4.84 inches
  • Shooting Modes:
    • Aperture-Priority
    • Manual
    • Programmed auto with flexible program
    • Shutter-Priority
  • Focus Type: Manual Focus
